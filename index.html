Welcome To Enyirich Farms

Where you get quality agricultural products you can trust

Enyirich Farm is an agricultural and an animal husbandry organization that produce and supply a diverse range of high-quality products, including mushrooms, poultry, poultry products, and even albino rats. Founded by a passionate team of farmers and animal enthusiasts, Enyirich farm is more than just a business; it's a labor of love. Mushrooms are among our flagship offerings, cultivated with care and expertise in our state-of-the-art facilities and so is our poultry and poultry products. Whether you're a culinary connoisseur or a health-conscious individual, you'll appreciate the freshness and flavor of our gourmet mushrooms and our well fed, heavy and healthy birds and fresh eggs, hand-picked and delivered straight to your table. ... In addition to mushrooms and poultry, we're proud to introduce our unique offering: albino rats. These fascinating creatures are not only adorable companions but also valuable research subjects for scientific and educational purposes. With their snow-white fur and inquisitive nature, our albino rats are sure to capture your heart. At Enyirich Farms, we're not just providers of agricultural products; we're stewards of the land and guardians of animal welfare.

Our Products.

Explore our fresh mushrooms, flavorful poultry, and adorable albino rat. Our farm's products offer something special for everyone.Each product is crafted with care and love.

Mushroom

Our hand-picked mushrooms aren't just delicious; they're also packed with nutrients and versatile in the kitchen. From adding depth to soups and stews to being the star ingredient in vegetarian dishes, mushrooms elevate your meals while offering numerous health benefits.

Poultry

Enjoy more than just fresh eggs with our poultry products. Our free-range chickens provide lean, flavorful meat that's perfect for a variety of dishes. Whether you're grilling, roasting, or frying, our poultry is a wholesome source of protein.

Albino Rat

Beyond their adorable appearance, albino rats offer companionship and joy to those seeking unique pets. Known for their intelligence and affectionate nature, these rats quickly become cherished members of the family.
